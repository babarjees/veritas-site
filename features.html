The Veritas product, end to end.

Six interlocking layers that turn a generic review widget into Pakistan's most credible trust signal — from order-bound tokens to a Karachi-based dispute desk.

The Gateway: verified-purchase only

Signed review tokens are issued at order fulfilment, so only a real buyer of that exact item can ever leave a review for it.

The Sentinel: Roman-Urdu NLP

Native Roman-Urdu, Urdu, and English sentiment models catch copy-paste bot rings and paid five-star templates other tools miss.

The Investigator: image forensics

Reverse-image search and EXIF analysis flag stolen product photos and recycled review media across competing storefronts.

The Jury: human moderation

A Karachi-based content-integrity team adjudicates every flagged review and runs the formal merchant dispute process.

Trust Score badge & widget

A live, on-storefront badge with a tap-to-verify drawer. Drop-in widget for Shopify, WooCommerce, and custom carts.

Merchant analytics & disputes

A console for review velocity, fraud flags, dispute SLAs, and conversion-lift attribution — exportable to your BI tool.
