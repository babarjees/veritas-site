Our Mission

Make every review on a Pakistani storefront one a real buyer wrote.

Trust is the bottleneck of Pakistani e-commerce. 75% of orders are still Cash-on-Delivery because shoppers refuse to prepay a brand they cannot verify. Veritas exists to close that gap — one verified-purchase review at a time — so honest merchants stop losing margin to RTO and bad-actor competitors stop hiding behind paid five-star ratings.

Our vision

A Pakistan where "Veritas Certified" is the default proof of trust online.

We want the green Veritas badge to sit next to the price on every Pakistani storefront — Shopify, WooCommerce, Daraz, Instagram, TikTok. When that becomes the cultural shorthand for "this seller is real, these reviews are real, this product matches the photo," cash-on-delivery stops being the only safe option, returns go down, and good merchants finally outcompete the imposters.
