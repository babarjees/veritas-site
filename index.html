Features

A four-layer defence against fake reviews.

From a verified-only review gateway and AI fraud detection to image forensics and human moderation — every review that carries the Veritas badge has earned it.

The Gateway: verified-purchase only.

No purchase, no review. Signed tokens are issued on order fulfilment so only real buyers can ever post a review for that product.

The Sentinel: AI fraud detection.

Roman-Urdu NLP plus behavioural graph analysis catches bot rings and copy-pasted reviews.

The Investigator: image & metadata forensics.

Reverse-image search and EXIF analysis flag stolen product photos and recycled review media.

The Jury: human moderation team.

A Karachi-based content-integrity team adjudicates flagged reviews and runs the formal dispute process for merchants.
